Well Baked Bread

Recipe by: Hugh Blackman

Prep Time

50 Mins

Serving

2 loaves

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of All-purporse Flour
  • 1 1/2 Cups Of White Sugar
  • 1 Cup Of Butter
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1/2 Teaspoon of salt
  • 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder

Tips

Grease pan before pouring in the batter, Bake at medium temperature
